Prologue - Pt. 4

Scene - Puff Town

As time passes and you start to worry about the princess, Pebble tugs your sleeve and guides you up a narrow trail along the hill. It is not far from the castle - hardly a secret at all. Yet when Pebble insists on bringing you here, you follow. At the top, you see her.

Princess Peridot sits alone, her back turned, ears swaying in the wind. You reach out a hand - then freeze. The view steals your breath.

The air shimmers with drifting pollen, glowing like small crystals. A faint wiff of magic stirs with the breeze. Below, the valley burns with color. Puff Castle rises against the horizon, its jeweled towers burning with the last light of day. Gold and violet shadows spill across the valey, like a dream painted in light. For a moment, even your thoughts fall silent.

Peridot

Come sit with me. Thanks for keeping Pebble company, by the way.

She looks so calm and serene you wouldn't have known she was the same princess you met earlier. You sit down beside her, while Pebble stays a few feet away, watching over her.

Peridot

Funny, isn't it? I ran away from that place... and here I am, staring at it.

You

That castle... it is beautiful.

Pebble

I like looking at it too. It makes you feel small, but also... wondering?

Pebble

They say it wasn't built by Puffs. Just look at the size of it! Those doors, the stairs. Even a Seeker like you is too small for it.

Pebble

It's been there for generations. Even the oldest Pufflings can't remember a time without it. They tell stories of their grandparents saying the same thing, as if the castle was already ancient back then.

Pebble

Yet it stood there for generations. Even the oldest Pufflings can't remember a time it wasn't there. And maybe that's why pufflings started making up stories. To explain what doesn't have an answer.

One of my favorite stories was about where Pufflings came from. The knight's captain used to tell it when I couldn't sleep. She'd say we were descended from dragons - great, fire-breathing beasts that ruled the world long ago. For them, a castle like that wouldn't have seemed so large. Some nights she would tell me the fire still sleeps in our hearts, waiting for the right Puffling to unlock it. The other knights laughed at me for clinging to a child's tale, but I never minded. It gave me something to reach for.

You

Who knows... The legends of dragons carried us Seekers across the world. And it led us here.

Peridot

But all you found were weak, little Pufflings...

You

It's true that at first many of us were disappointed. But little by little, that changed. You laughed with us, trained beside us, stumbled and stood again. And in those moments, we realized...that the dream isn't over yet. The path ahead is long, but we'll walk it together.

Pebble

That is why I like Seekers. You're brave and you never give up.

Pebble unsheathes his sword, and raises it high.

Pebble

That's why I won't give up either! One day... I'll grow stronger and unlock the fire burning inside me! I'll be even stronger than a dragon!

Peridot

Oh come on, Pebble~ What would you even do with that power?

The three of you sit in the small wind, lauhging and watching the ring above the castle turn. No more words are needed, but in your hearts, you're already looking far beyond towards a world full of dreams, of questions and answers waiting to be found.

By the time you return, the sun is setting. Light gathers on the towers as if guiding the princess home.

King

Hmph! About time you got back! It is nearly nightfall—and those guards still have not closed the gates!

King

Hmph, here. You must be hungry after wandering all day. What a coincidence—we just happened to have pudding. I definitely did not ask the chef to make it.

Peridot

I am not upset about that anymore. That pudding was spoiled anyway. Serves you right for eating it—bleh.

King

You little—ugh! My stomach started hurting the moment you said that…

Peridot laughs, bright and unguarded. Pebble’s shoulders relax.

Pebble

She is laughing again… That means she forgives you.

Queen

It is not much… but please accept this.

Inside the pouch lie colorful crystals that smell faintly sweet.

Queen

They are candies. I made them myself.

You

(They look like gemstones… but they feel warm.)

Before you leave the castle, you say your farewells.

Pebble

One day I will become a real knight! I will protect Princess Peridot!

Peridot

Pebble! Stop saying embarrassing things!

You

I have only begun as a Seeker. Let us grow stronger together.

Peridot

Thanks for today, Seeker. Besides Pebble, you are the only one who listened to my dream. Next time, you tell me yours.

You

Next time—without sneaking out.

Their laughter follows you down the road.

Night has fallen by the time you return to the Guild Hall. A tall man in a white cloak sits where Aria should be. A Puff sleeps on his head, rising and falling with each breath.

Guild Master

Oh! You are the new recruit. Sorry I was out—Aria covered for me. Already finished a mission?

You explain. He listens, amused.

Guild Master

So she tricked you. I never told her to send you. Puff Castle errands are usually formalities. No rewards.

Guild Master

She lost her Puff partner recently. Work is all she has. Do not be hard on her.

Guild Master

As for you: tomorrow is different. A real mission. Rest well. We leave at sunrise.

Before sleep, you stop at Poki’s stall.

You

Thanks for earlier. I want to trade something.

Poki

What?! Candy? You trying to scam me?

Momo

Meow~

You explain you received the Queen’s candy and want a gift for Aria.

You

This is all I have from my first mission. Please help me.

Poki

…Heh. Aria the Lone Flame, huh? That is worth more than enough. Here—your change. You overpaid.

You

Thank you, Poki!

Momo

Meow~

Poki

That candy is packed with magic. Worth ten times what you think.

Momo sighs a long, bored sigh.

That night, you fall asleep in a small guest room at the guild. A breeze slips through the window, carrying the soft, crystal-sweet scent of dark.

If you have a Puff partner, it curls at your side. A tiny courier Puff takes the gift from your sill, bound for Aria.

You close your eyes—and think of Pebble’s oath, Peridot’s smile, the Queen’s quiet gift, and the distance in Aria’s gaze. Tomorrow you leave the capital to walk the true path of a Seeker.

End of Prologue - Pt. 4
