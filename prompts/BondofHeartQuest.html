Quest Overview

A Puffling cannot transform into a human form through its own power alone. Transformation can only occur when a Puffling and a Seeker form a complete bond.

That bond is forged through:

  • Feelings
  • Acceptance
  • And a Heart Crystal

The Heart Crystal is a crystal freely given by a Puffling to a Seeker.
Normally, it is nothing more than a beautiful heart-shaped crystal, a manifestation of the Puffling‚Äôs feelings toward someone.

However, when a true Bond is formed, the Heart Crystal releases a dormant power, One that allows the Puffling to transform.

Even so,
There is no right or wrong reason for a bond to exist.
Each bond is defined solely by the story of that pair.
