Act 2 - Ch 7: The Beginning of the Journey

Scene — Puff Outskirt

The supply convoy slowly moved away from the Puff Capital.

You sat quietly, watching the towering crystal walls of the city gradually fade into the distance behind you.

The convoy consisted of Dango, Mochi, Poki, Momo, you, and your Puff partner. A few other Puffs had joined the trip as well, helping carry the cargo.

You glanced at them as they slept comfortably atop the stacks of supplies, completely at ease.

Then the wagon passed through something so thin it was almost impossible to notice.

A faint chill brushed across your skin.

You turned around and noticed a pale blue magical curtain spreading across the sky around the city.

You

Was that… a magic barrier just now?

You

I never noticed it before.

Poki nodded.

Poki

Yep. That’s the barrier protecting Puff’s capital.

Poki

It’s similar to the big barrier that covers the entire Puff Kingdom. This one keeps magical beasts from entering the city.

Poki

The Puff King made it. But Puff and Seekers can pass through easily

Poki

Pretty convenient, eh?

As the wagon passed through the barrier, the air around you shifted slightly.

Mochi and Dango began pulling the wagon faster.

The wooden wheels rolled over the stone road in a steady rhythm.

The road stretched out from the capital, running through fields and gentle hills.

Crystals scattered along the roadside reflected the morning sunlight in small sparkling flashes. Some leaves shifted color faintly whenever the wind passed through them.

You

Hey Poki, earlier you mentioned something about Behemoth Puff, right?

Poki stood on top of a wooden crate with her arms crossed before flashing a wide grin.

Poki

Alright~!

She clapped her hands once.

Poki

Welcome to Poki’s Traveling Classroom!

Several Puff riding in the convoy turned to look at her. Some of them looked excited, as if they were about to hear a story.

Poki

During the trip, I’ll teach you all about Puff~

Poki

There are actually many different kinds of Puff.

Poki

The ones with two arms and two legs, like most Puffs we see, are called Dragon Puff!

One Puff on the wagon quickly raised its hand.

Puff

I’m a Dragon Puff!

Poki

I know, I know~!

Poki laughed.

Poki

They’re the most common kind. When people talk about Puff, they usually mean these.

Poki

But there are also three subspecies.

Poki

Behemoth, Wyvern, and Leviathan.

She pointed toward Dango and Mochi, pulling the wagon ahead.

Poki

Those two are Behemoth Puff.

Poki

Behemoths have four legs, large horns, and incredible strength.

Dango turned his head proudly and nodded.
Mochi puffed out his chest slightly, as if showing off his muscles.

Poki

Puff with wings instead of arms are called Wyvern, and the ones with fins and fish-like tails are called Leviathan.

Poki

We don’t see them very often though.

Poki

They prefer very specific environments.

Poki

Wyverns like deep forests, while Leviathans stay near water.

Mochi

While I like staying near the food!!

Mochi added casually

Poki leaned toward you with a curious grin.

Poki

So what kind of Puff is your partner, I wonder?

The wagon continued down the stone road.

You looked at the road thoughtfully.

You

Did the Seekers build these roads?

Poki shook her head.

Poki

We just repaired them.

You

So…

You

That means there are people who used this road a long time ago?

Poki shrugged.

Poki

Well, Puffs doesn’t really need roads

Poki

So someone—some group before us—must have built them.

Poki

Who knows?

You looked down the road, stretching far ahead.
This path seemed to hold more history than you had expected.

Poki jumped down from the crate and unfolded a small map.

Poki

Now then, let’s talk about our mission.

She pointed to two locations on the map.

Poki

We’re heading to meet two Puff.

Poki

Sugar of the Sleeping Forest, and Leo the Immortal.

Poki explained.

Poki

Sugar is a Leviathan Puff and an alchemist.

Poki

And Leo is a Behemoth Puff who forges weapons.

Poki

This convoy was carrying

  • herbs
  • ores
  • foods

to deliver to them.

Poki pointed at the cargo, causing a Puff secretly nibbling on a carrot to jump in surprise.

Poki

Got it? These will be used as materials to make medicine, weapons, and other equipment.

Poki

Once everything is ready, we’ll bring all of it to the front lines.

She traced the route across the map and shrugged.

Poki

Put simply… if this convoy doesn’t make it there, the front line is guaranteed to lose.

Those words reminded you of what the Guild Master had said.

The main artery of the battlefield.

It seemed he hadn’t been exaggerating.

You had traveled far from Puff Capital now.
The forest along the road grew dense and thicker. A thin mist began spreading through the trees.

Poki

We’re here

Poki

From here on, we enter the Sleeping Forest.

Poki added

Poki

They call it that because people say once you enter, you might never find your way back.

You looked toward the dense forest ahead.
After a moment, you asked quietly.

You

The front lines are fighting in the Darkzone, right?

You

How far is the Darkzone from Puff Capital?

Poki fell silent for a moment.
Then she answered quietly.

Poki

Not far enough to feel safe.

She looked ahead.

Poki

Darkzone X is the closest Darkzone to Puff Capital.

As you know, Darkzones are areas Seekers haven’t been able to fully explore yet.

whether because the terrain is too dangerous, the mysteries are too difficult to solve, or the monsters are simply too vicious

She paused.

Poki

Like the Insect Tribe.

Poki

They never used to leave the Darkzone.

Poki

But this time they’re moving in organized groups, heading straight toward Puff Capital.

Poki

That thin barrier we saw earlier… probably won’t be able to stop them.

You

So the insects really are that dangerous…

Poki sighed softly.

Poki

Aria fought them once.

She hesitated briefly before continuing

Poki

She entered Darkzone X and fought the insects there.

Poki

And… she….

Poki

Lost her Puff partner

You froze

You

Why did she go there?

Poki answered slowly.

Poki

All we know is that she was searching for some kind of medicine

Poki

Aria prefers working alone with only her Puff partner

Poki

That’s why people call her the Lone Flame

The wagon wheels continued rolling steadily over the road. Poki stared into the distance.

Poki

On that day, she came back alone, badly wounded

Poki

Even though she entered the Darkzone without permission, it cost her dearly. But because of that, we learned about the insect movement

Poki

You could call it a luck within misfortune

Poki

Since then… she’s refused to bond with another Puff

You sat quietly, unsure what to say.

Poki

Maybe…

Poki

…part of her still believes that her Puff is out there somewhere.

The wagon continued forward through the thickening mist. The air had grown quiet.

Then, suddenly, one Puff woke up and sniffed the air sharply.

Puff

Something smells strange…

Other Puff sat up, looking around nervously.

Momo’s ears twitched.

Momo

Meow…

Poki

I feel it too, Momo

Poki said quietly.
Dango and Mochi immediately stopped pulling the wagon. Their massive bodies tensed without needing any orders.

Suddenly—

The bushes ahead shook violently.
Several dark shapes burst out from the forest.

Magical beasts.

They looked like wolves.
Their eyes glowed, and their long claws shimmered with magic as they growled.

Poki

Uh oh…

Poki

Looks like they smelled the food.

You

Do you mean the cargo or us, Poki?!

The magical beasts lunged toward the convoy.

Dango slammed into one of them, sending it flying.

Mochi swung his horns and hurled another one away.

The two Behemoth Puffs fought fiercely.

Their massive bodies clashed like two moving mountains.

The smaller Puff riding with the convoy squealed and quickly huddled together into a trembling ball atop the wagon.

You and your Puff partner rushed to defend the convoy.

The magical beasts attacked from every direction.

Their claws glowed with magic.
Even a slight graze on the tree left deep gouges in the bark.

If those claws hit you directly…
it wouldn’t end well.

In the chaos
One beast, larger than the others.

Snuck behind Poki.

You saw it for a split second.

You

Poki, behind you!

You shouted and rushed forward.

But then—

Momo’s eyes widened.

Purple magic flashed.

A mysterious eye-shaped magic sigil appeared.
The beast froze mid-air, as if being held in place by an invisible force.

Poki laughed.

Poki

See?

She proudly rubbed Momo’s head.

Poki

My Momo is amazing

Then she added quickly

Poki

But even without Momo, I could’ve beaten it bare-handed!

She clenched her fist

Poki

Hya! Poki Punch!

With the leader frozen, the other beasts hesitated. They looked at each other uncertainly.

But after Mochi launched another one sky-high with his horns, The rest began retreating

Poki

Oh… so you’re the leader, huh?

Poki said, walking toward the frozen beast.

Poki

Got you!

She grabbed a large loaf of bread from the cargo and stuffed it into the beast’s mouth.

Poki

Alright, alright. Back to the forest you go

Momo released the spell.
The beast looked confused for a moment before running off into the forest after the others.

Poki cupped her hands around her mouth and shouted after them.

Poki

Don’t forget to tell your friends that bread tastes good too!

You stared at Poki in disbelief.

You

…..

You

So…

You

do you actually need an escort?

Poki smiled.

Poki

Well~ the more, the merrier, right?

She answered in her usual playful tone.
The Puffs, who had been hugging each other like a ball, slowly peeked their heads out.

Puff

We helped protect the cargo too!

Puff

Yeah! Nothing’s missing!

After that,
The convoy continued peacefully.
No magical beasts appeared again.

Poki took out her Keystone, letting its faint magical light guide the way.

The dense forest gradually gave way to a wetland.
Tall trees grew sparse.
Wide marshes replaced them.
Thin mist drifted slowly across the water.
Frogs croaked somewhere in the distance.

Poki pointed ahead.

Poki

We’re here

A mysterious swamp appeared before you.

Poki

Sugar’s home

Our supply convoy had reached its first destination. ________________
