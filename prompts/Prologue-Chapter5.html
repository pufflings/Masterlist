Act 1 - Ch 5: The Beginning of the Journey

Scene — Puff Town

You returned to Puff Castle just as the sun was setting. The last light of day caught the tips of the towers, as if guiding the princess back home. The road shimmered with crystal reflections cast by the castle walls.

Normally, by this time of day, the princess would have fallen asleep halfway through the walk, and Pebble would end up carrying her the rest of the way.

But today… she walked on ahead, a bright smile on her face.

King

Hah! Take it. You must be hungry after running off all day. Today’s snack just happens to be pudding. What a coincidence!

King

I, uh… didn’t tell the cooks to make it or anything!

Peridot turned her face away slightly with a teasing smile.

Peridot

I’m not mad about that anymore~ That pudding was spoiled anyway! Serves you right, whoever ate it — bleh~!

King

Why, you little—!
Ugh, my stomach hurts just thinking about it!

Peridot laughed, her voice ringing through the grand hall like chimes.

Pebble

Seeing her laugh like that… I’d say that means she’s forgiven you, Your Majesty.

Queen Puff appeared behind them with a gentle smile. She held out a small, simple-looking cloth pouch for you.

Queen

It’s not much… but please, take this.

Inside the pouch were tiny shimmering crystals, glowing faintly in different hues and carrying a soft, sweet scent.

Queen

They’re candies. I made them myself.

For a moment, you almost thought you’d been handed a priceless treasure. The colours sparkled like gems, yet instead of the cold touch of crystal, they felt warm, like candlelight. A small gift, filled with warmth and sincerity.

Pebble and peridot follow you to the castle gate to send you off.

Pebble

Someday… I’ll become a real knight! So I can protect Princess Peridot!

Peridot

Oh, Pebble~! That’s so embarrassing—what are you even saying?!

You

I’m just starting out on my own path, too. Let’s grow stronger together.

You smiled softly.

Peridot

Thank you for today, Seeker.
Other than Pebble, you’re the only one who’s ever listened to my dreams.

Peridot

Next time… you have to tell me all about yours, in detail, alright?

You

Deal. But next time, hopefully without you having to sneak out of the castle, eh?.

Their laughter echoed through the evening air as a cool breeze swept past. The last rays of sunlight faded from the sky, and the soft laughter of the Puffs trailed behind you as you walked away.

By the time you returned to The Hatchery, the sky was already dark. Just before you could enter to report your mission, you heard voices arguing from inside the Guild Master’s office.

Aria(?)

Oh, come on, old man! Don’t be so strict! Now’s not the time to worry about nitpicky rules!

???

Those rules exist for our safety — and you know that better than anyone. You know how dangerous it is to take on missions without a Puff.

Aria(?)

Stop treating me like a child already! You expect me to sit here guarding the nest while everyone else is out risking their lives?

???

You don’t realise how much we need skilled people like you right now. If you’d just partner with a new Puff, I’d send you out there in a heartbeat, girl.

Aria(?)

I know that…!

Just then, Aria burst out of the room, irritation written all over her face. She froze for a moment when she saw you standing there.

Time seemed to have stopped. But before you could even think of what to say, she stepped forward, smiled faintly, and tapped your chest lightly with the back of her hand.

Aria

…Til the day we find magic — see you around, rookie.

Then she walked away.
That familiar phrase, the one Seekers had used since the days before the discovery of the Puff Kingdom, carried many meanings. Even now, when everyone knows that magic exists, the words are still spoken, time and time again.

Because it’s the phrase that brought us here… and will carry us onward into tomorrow. The words that came from Aria’s lips felt strangely powerful.

You took a deep breath and stepped into the room. There, you finally met the real Guild Master for the first time.

A tall man in a white cloak paced back and forth, his brows slightly furrowed. He looked calm and composed, yet not intimidating; his eyes held a playful spark beneath his serious expression. Resting on top of his head was a Puff, fast asleep.

Guild Master

Oh! The new recruit! We finally meet at last.

Guild Master

Every time I talk with that girl, I end up with a headache. That’s why I’ve got this Puff to help me out.

He pointed to the Puff perched on his head.

Guild Master

Sorry about that. I had my hands full, so I asked Aria to handle things for me.

He squinted slightly, studying you.

Guild Master

Hm? You’ve already completed a mission?

You nodded and told him everything from start to finish. The Guild Master chuckled softly, stroking his chin.

Guild Master

Ha! I see now… You got tricked by Aria. I never ordered anyone to start their first mission.

Guild Master

That little troublemaker, though she’s not that young anymore…

Guild Master

Missions from Puff Castle are usually minor errands. Hardly ever come with any reward. It’s part of an old agreement between the guild and the Puff Kingdom. Think of it as… paying rent for staying here.

You gave a faint, awkward smile. For a moment, the Guild Master fell silent, as though weighing his next words. His voice softened when he spoke again.

Guild Master

Aria recently lost her Puff partner in battle… She probably hasn’t had the heart to take on much since then.

Guild Master

Don’t hold it against her, alright?

You paused for a moment at the news.

You didn’t believe she sent you out just because of that, but you nodded understandingly. From what you’d overheard earlier, she seemed like someone who would keep moving forward no matter what.

You found yourself wishing to talk with her again someday.

Guild Master

Aria probably already had you fill out the registration form, but if that’s all, your first day at the guild must’ve been rather dull.

Guild Master

So—how about you tell me your dragon legend?

The question sounded whimsical, yet his tone was sincerely curious.

Each Seeker joins the guild with their own beliefs about the legend of dragons. Some believe meeting one grants immortality; others say it grants a wish. But no one will truly know until the day a dragon is found. And so, though they share the same goal, their paths are filled with countless dreams and possibilities.

Guild Master

I’m terrible with faces, you know… but stories? Those, I never forget.

He looked up again, smiling — his expression calm but resolute.

Guild Master

And one more thing, this might be a bit sudden, but we’ve got a major mission coming up.

Guild Master

This will be your first real mission. Be ready — we set out at sunrise!

Guild Master

Welcome to the guild officially, little chick.

He clapped your shoulder with surprising strength and laughed heartily.

You left the room, saying your goodbyes to the guild members and the helpful guild Puffs who had assisted in your first task.

The lights in the main hall dimmed, leaving only the faint, shimmering glow of the Navigator crystal at the heart of the guild.

The night air was cool, but you decided to stop by Poki’s stall before heading back to your room.

You

Hey, lucky me, you’re still open.

You

Thanks for everything earlier. My first mission… I guess it went pretty well?

The job at Puff Castle doesn’t officially count, but still — I’m a Seeker now, so… yeah.

You smile sheepishly

Poki

Heehee~ Since everything turned out fine, how about you buy something from my shop as thanks, hmm?

You

Actually… I came to trade something.

You pulled out the small cloth pouch and showed her the crystal candy inside. Before you could say another word, Poki frowned.

Poki

What!? Trying to swindle me with candy!? You think I’m that gullible, huh!?

Momo

Meow~

Poki puffed up her cheeks in annoyance.

You smiled awkwardly and quickly explained that the candy was a gift from the Queen, and you wanted to trade it for something — a present for Aria.

You

It’s the only thing I got from my first mission. Please, Poki, could you help me with this?

Poki’s expression softened immediately. She seemed to understand without a word.

Poki

…Hmph. Aria the Lone Flame, huh? I see.

Poki

Then.... Something like this… is more than enough.

She carefully placed a small item into a box and took only a single piece of candy in exchange.

Poki

Here — your change. You overpaid.

You

Thanks, Poki!

You ran off with a smile.

Momo

Meow~

Poki

What? Me, softhearted? Don’t be ridiculous, Momo — you wouldn’t understand.

Poki

That candy came from the Puff Queen herself! It’s packed with magic power worth more than enough for any deal!

Poki

Anyone foolish enough to eat it would be out of their mind! Hahaha~!

Momo deeply sighed, her soft sound fading into the cool night breeze.

That night, you lay down on the small bed in your guild quarters. A gentle breeze drifted through the window, carrying the faint scent of magical crystals shimmering in the night sky.

A tiny Post Puff, brimming with enthusiasm, arrived at your window to collect the parcel you had prepared for Aria. The tiny satchel it carried somehow fit the large package with ease, like a small miracle in itself. Then, with a flutter of wings, it vanished into the darkness.

Your Puff partner nestled beside you, breathing softly. The steady rhythm of its breath lulled everything around you into stillness.

You closed your eyes.

In your mind lingered the laughter of the Puffs you've met today, Pebble’s determined eyes, Peridot’s bright smile — and the lingering story of Aria that still weighed on your heart.

Tomorrow morning, you will have to leave the capital, venturing into the lands unseen, onto the true path of a Seeker.

And this… was the beginning of an adventure that might change your life — or perhaps… the world itself.

For Seekers, never stop moving forward, not merely to find the dragons, but because the search itself is what keeps their hearts alive. As long as the journey continues, dragons exist.

You

And Til the day we find magic… goodnight, Puff Kingdom.

End of Act 1 - Ch 5: The Beginning of the Journey
