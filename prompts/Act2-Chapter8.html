Act 2 - Ch 8: The Beginning of the Journey

Scene — Sleeping Forest

The convoy continued moving slowly through the swamp.

The water surface was perfectly calm. Clear bubbles rose from the depths every now and then, drifting lazily upward. When caught with the sunlight, it reflected soft rainbow colors.

Poki

We’ve entered the Pearl Marsh

Her voice sounded noticeably brighter.

Ahead of them lay a swamp, its surface clear and still like a mirror. Soft pastel mist drifted gently upward from the water while hundreds of bubbles floated through the air.

The Puffs riding on the wagon grew excited. They leapt and pounced at the bubbles drifting past their faces. Some even bat them back and forth like toys.

Giggles spread out across the wagon.

The tense atmosphere from the earlier battle slowly faded away.

Poki

Beautiful, right? This is Sugar’s territory

Poki said as she watched a bubble float over and stick onto Momo’s nose.

Poki

Leviathans like to stay close to water

Poki

They’re incredible swimmers, and they can even use magic to control the water around them

She gestured toward the surrounding swamp.

Poki

That’s why this place looks like this

Then Poki raised a finger.

Poki

But that’s not the only thing Sugar’s good at, she’s the best potion maker I’ve ever met!

Poki

Most of the potions sold at Poki Shop come from her

She gave a small, dry laugh.

Poki

But well…

Poki

Sometimes she also mixes some very strange experimental potions and makes me test them

Poki seemed to remember something, then quickly added

Poki

Still… every single one of Sugar’s potions is amazing

She quickly lifted her glowing Keystone again
At that moment, the swamp ahead slowly parted, forming a narrow path for the wagon.

Poki

If you accidentally step on that shiny water, you’ll slip and fall flat on your face.

Poki laughed

Poki

Sugar designed it that way to keep magical beasts away.

You looked at the shimmering swamp

You

With all those bubbles… it kind of looks like a giant bathtub

Poki chuckled softly.

Poki

I guess it does

Poki

Sugar likes quiet places, so she came all the way out here to live alone

Before long, Sugar’s house came into view.

The convoy stopped in front of a small and cozy-looking house. Its walls were made from enormous seashells adorned with lustrous pearls. The house stood in the middle of the swamp, where the water was so clear you could see all the way to the bottom.

The moment the wagon stopped, Dango and Mochi immediately flopped onto their backs.

Mochi

Fwooo—!

They waved their legs in the air while playing with floating bubbles, rolling happily across the ground.

Poki walked up and knocked on the door.

Knock Knock Knock

Poki

Sugar! I’m here to deliver the supplies!

No response.

Poki

Sugar~!

Still nothing.

Poki knocked again, louder this time.

Poki

Sugaaaar!

Silence

Poki sighed.

Poki

Well, I’m going in

She shrugged and opened the door.

Inside the house were shelves filled with colorful glassware and potion bottles. Bubbles drifted gently throughout the room.

And in the center of it all—

A Puff floated inside a giant bubble.

Her long translucent blue fins swayed softly in the air as she hugged a pillow, fast asleep.

Poki walked closer.
Then she poked the bubble with her Keystone.

Pop!

The bubble burst.
Sugar spun through the air, flipping gracefully like a fish swimming in water.

Then she slowly opened her eyes.

Sugar

Mmm…

Sugar

Oh… Poki…

Sugar

Did you bring ingredients… or food today…?

Poki

Both!

Poki laughed.

Poki

This is my new friend. A Seeker who’s helping escort our supplies

Sugar yawned widely.
Then she waved her fin lazily.
A few small bubbles floated over and formed seats in front of you.

Sugar

Hello there, new Seeker

Sugar

Have a seat… My bubble furniture is quite sturdy… and bouncy

Poki

Up all night making potions again?

Poki

And how’s Froggy doing?

Sugar rubbed her eyes.
Then she pointed to a large glass jar.

Inside it, a big green frog sat soaking in water.

Momo flew over immediately. The frog slowly opened its eyes.

Froggy

Ribbit…

Sugar

Thank you for bringing the supplies all the way here. I’m Sugar, the potion maker of this marsh

Then she pointed at the frog.

Froggy

And that’s Froggy, my assistant

It was a slightly strange sight—
a Puff living together with other animals.

But outside Puff Capital, everything seemed a little strange anyway.

Poki began explaining the situation on the front lines—the emergency, and the insect tribe from the Darkzone.

While the Puffs outside unloaded the cargo, Sugar listened.

She nodded.

…nodded.

…nodded again.

And then—

She fell asleep.

Face-first into a floating bubble.
Poki rushed over and shook her.

Poki

Hey! Wake up! Sugar!

Sugar jolted awake.

Sugar

Huh?! I’m listening!

She quickly stood up.

Sugar

Mhm! Perfect timing, actually

Sugar

I just finished brewing a new potion, A healing formula. Works faster than the old one

Sugar

Just needs to be bottled

She proudly showed off the new potion she had brewed.

But as she reached for an empty bottle on the shelf—

Sugar fell asleep again.

Her body tipped forward and plunged straight into a giant boiling cauldron.

Splash!

You & Poki

Whoa! Sugar!

You and Poki hurriedly pulled the sleepy Puff out of the pot, now covered in potion.

Sugar

Cough cough—!

Then she laughed awkwardly.

Sugar

Hehe… my fur is waterproof. Don’t worry

You stared at the clumsy Puff with concern and a bit of confusion.
How had she managed to survive out here all alone?

Sugar

I’ll make some tea for you. Just a moment

She disappeared into the back of the house and soon returned with fragrant herbal tea.

Sugar

Alright, let’s continue

After that cup of tea, Sugar didn’t fall asleep again.

Once she understood the full situation, Sugar looked worried.

Sugar

Those ancient insects…

Sugar

They’re extremely aggressive, but they normally never leave their territory, and now they’re heading toward Puff Capital…

Sugar

That’s not normal at all

You asked.

You

When you say ancient…

You

Do you mean they’ve been around for a long time?

Sugar nodded

Sugar

A very long time, they existed before Puff ever did.

Poki added

Poki

According to legend, they date back to the Dragon Era

Poki

And some stories say they once fought against the dragon race and were defeated… but they still managed to injure the dragons badly

Sugar

Those dragon legends, huh…

She fell silent for a moment.
Poki laughed.

Poki

Puffs don’t really believe those stories, do they?

Poki

But Seekers like us are the ones who chased those legends all the way here

Sugar shook her head slightly

Sugar

It’s not that we don’t believe them

Sugar

We don’t really know the true origins of our own kind either…

Sugar

But for us to believe that Puffs like us were once dragons is a bit…

She laughed softly.

Sugar

And besides

Sugar

If that legend were true….

Sugar gazed out the window.

Sugar

The god who cursed dragons and turned them into Puffs

Sugar

…must have been a very cruel god

Sugar

I mean… wasn’t there a better way to deal with it?

Momo

Meow…. (If a god had that much power, couldn’t they just make the dragons behave instead?)

The frog in the jar blew a stream of bubbles.

Froggy

Ribbit! (Exactly! Cruel and stupid too, that useless god!)

You

Yeah… that’s true. Why go that far?

Poki

Huh?

Momo

Meow?? (Huh??)

Froggy

Ribbit??? (Huh???)

Suddenly—the room fell silent.
Sugar’s eyes widened. Poki’s jaw dropped.
Momo and the frog both turned to stare at you.

Poki

Wait.

Poki

You… understand them?

You blinked.

You

They… were talking, weren’t they?

Sugar quickly floated closer, her face nearly touching yours.

Sugar

Froggy doesn’t speak human language…

Sugar

and Momo use a secret code with Poki.

Poki grabbed your Keystone and examined it.
It was glowing more brightly than before.

Sugar

I see…

Sugar

All Seekers can use a little bit of magic, but that power doesn’t come from them

Sugar pointed at the Keystone.

Sugar

The Keystone responds to its owner and lends them a small amount of magic

Sugar

But a power like this…

Poki

Mine is navigation magic

Poki

It helps me find people or objects. That’s how I guided us here

Outside the window, Dango and Mochi peeked in curiously.

Sugar murmured

Sugar

You are a Seeker who has an ability related to communication

Sugar

…..A very rare kind of magic

You

But what good is that??

You

Puffs can already talk to humans just fine. Momo just chooses to speak in code with Poki.

Sugar shook her head.

Sugar

It’s not that you understand their language. But you can directly sense others' thoughts and feelings.

Sugar

But it looks like you still need a lot of practice.

Sugar

You can’t control it very well yet, can you?

You turned back toward Momo and the frog

They sounded normal again.

Poki smiled.

Poki

Everyone develops their abilities at their own pace, how strong it becomes depends on how much you train

You tried listening again several times.

But nothing happened.

You

Aw… I was kind of hoping for a more dramatic kind of magic.

You chuckled awkwardly, half smiling, half staring into space like a crazy person.

Once all the potions had been loaded onto the wagon, the convoy was prepared to leave again.

Everyone said goodbye to Sugar.

Poki jumped up and sat on one of the wooden crates. She stroked her chin thoughtfully.

Poki

I wonder how I can make use of this new kid…

Momo looked at you, then she said.

Momo

Meow.

And this time—

You understood. ________________
