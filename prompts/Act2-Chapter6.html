Act 2 - Ch 6: The Bells of Departure

Scene — Guild Square

The guild bell rang out through the early morning air.

A thin layer of mist drifted lazily across the cold stone ground as the first rays of sunlight filtered through the crystal spires atop the buildings.

Seekers gradually gathered in the courtyard.
Some carried weapons over their shoulders, others carefully checked their gear with tense expressions.

In a stressful atmosphere, the Puff also has a serious look on their face as they line up to be inspected by their Seekers to assess their Physical condition and readiness. A few Puff puffed out their chests proudly to appear strong, even though the tips of their tails trembled slightly.

The mixture of excited and nervous chatter slowly faded as he stepped forward.

The Guild Master walked to the front. The familiar smile still lingered at the corner of his lips, but his expression was far more serious than it had been on the first day you met him.

Guild Master

Everyone—I’ll keep this brief and to the point

Guild Master

Right now, our front lines are facing an invasion… from the Insects

A quiet wave of whispers spread across the courtyard.

You

Insects…?

You muttered to yourself uncertainly.

Guild Master

The Insect Clans are not ordinary magical beasts

Guild Master

They are an ancient race with their own language, culture, and social systems. They usually live within the Darkzone, a dangerous region we have yet to fully explore

Guild Master

But recently… They’ve begun moving outside their territory far more frequently than before

He paused for a moment before speaking again, his voice firmer

Guild Master

And according to the information we have….

Guild Master

They are heading straight for the capital of Puff Kingdom

A stir spreads through the crowd.

Many Seekers knew how dangerous the insects were; they were on an entirely different level from the magical beasts usually encountered within Puff Kingdom.

Even the Puff, though they didn’t fully understand the situation, could sense the tension around them. Some curled up nervously, while others clung tighter to their Seekers’ arms.

Guild Master

And that brings us to today’s mission

Guild Master

We will reinforce the front lines and stop them before it’s too late.

He quickly began issuing orders.

Guild Master

Send word to all Seekers across Puff Kingdom. Gather immediately at the front lines—Darkzone Sector X

Guild Master

Till the day we find magic—good luck to you all

The Guild Master moved through the crowd, assigning roles and organizing the groups.

Guild Master

You—send messages through the long-distance relay

Guild Master

And you, take command of the medical unit

Then he stopped in front of you.

Guild Master

And as for you… I have an important mission

Your heart raced.
An important mission…?

Guild Master

You’ll escort Poki’s supply convoy

You

……Huh?

The words slipped out of your mouth almost instantly.

For a moment, the surrounding area fell silent. One Puff tilted its head and made a small, curious noise.

Curious Puff

…A food… convoy?

The Guild Master let out a quiet sigh.

Guild Master

I know that look in your eyes really well—just like Aria’s

Guild Master

I know you want to go fight

Guild Master

But the front line needs supplies, medicine, and weapons… just as much as it needs warriors

Guild Master

If this convoy doesn’t arrive, then no matter how skilled the fighters are, the outcome will be the same

You still felt a little confused.
Was he just trying to reassure you because you were new?
Is that why you weren’t going with them?

Before you could say anything, he stepped forward and gently placed a hand on your shoulder.

Guild Master

I’ve noticed you connect with Puff better than most people

Guild Master

Maybe it’s just my intuition… but something about you feels different

Guild Master

Your abilities… will definitely be useful for this mission

The seriousness and expectation in his voice made it clear this wasn’t a task given lightly

You

Thank you, Guild Master… I won’t let you down

He grinned, showing his teeth in his usual style.

Guild Master

Heh—now that’s more like it

Guild Master

This mission is basically the main artery of the battlefield, you know

Suddenly, a familiar cheerful voice rang out from behind.

Poki

Whoa~ the main artery, huh?

Poki

That sounds pretty grand~♪

Poki appeared, pushing a small cart piled high with packages. Several Puff bustled around behind her, helping push and carry boxes.

Poki

Anyway, I’m counting on you, rookie~

Momo sat atop the cart, looking as indifferent as ever before, softly greeting you.

Momo

Meow~

The Guild Master looked at the scene before him and let out a quiet sigh, a mix of relief and concern.

Guild Master

I’m counting on you, Poki

Poki climbed onto a wooden crate and lightly thumped her chest.

Poki

Don’t worry~

Poki

I guard these supplies just like I guard my shop goods! Hehehe~

Poki

And we’ve got our talented rookie here, too. This’ll be easy!

Momo let out a long “Meoow~,” sounding almost like a sigh on behalf of the convoy.

You paused—
For a moment, it almost felt like you understood what that meow meant… maybe you were just imagining it.

You looked toward the Guild Master’s back as he walked away.

Soft sunlight and the scent of breakfast drifted through the air.

A bright morning like this…
Would it really be the beginning of our battle?

You turned back and followed Poki toward the convoy.

A massive wagon stood ready, loaded nearly to the brim.

You

A wagon, Poki? What’s going to pull it? There aren’t any cows or horses here

???

We are!

Two large white Puff bounced forward to greet you.

Poki

These are Dango and Mochi—our convoy’s wagon pullers! They’re really amazing!

Dango and Mochi puffed out their chests proudly.

You looked at them more closely.
Their bodies were unlike normal Puffs. They look stronger and sturdier, and they walk on four legs.

Poki

They’re Behemoths. I’ll explain more along the way

Poki

Let’s go!

Momo hopped down and helped push Poki as she climbed skillfully onto the wagon.

You took a deep breath.

You

Okay…

You

Let’s make sure these supplies reach the front line safely

Poki grinned widely.

Poki

Great~!
Puff Kingdom’s Convoy of Hope is now departing~♪

Mochi scraped his feet against the ground repeatedly as red magic slowly wrapped around the feet and horns of both Puff.

Dango cast a body-expansion spell.
Their forms grew larger—until they were nearly the size of a horse.

Mochi

Alright… ready, Dango?

Mochi asked with a wide smile.
Dango chuckled softly.

Dango

Been ready since before the sun was up!

Mochi continued the line as if it were a song

Mochi

On the mountain, the stars feel near ~♪

Dango nodded.

Dango

In the river, the moon shines clear!

Together they sang:

Together

Though the road ahead is long, we have no fear,
for we will always have each other here!~♪

The massive wagon began moving forward, pulled by just two Puff. Their strength was impressive, but their magic-enhanced power was even more astonishing.

So this is another way to use magic… You murmured to yourself.

Morning sunlight shone over the convoy as Dango and Mochi continued singing cheerfully.

You pulled your Puff a little closer.

And just like that—
A new journey had begun.

End of Act 2 - Ch 6: The Bells of Departure
